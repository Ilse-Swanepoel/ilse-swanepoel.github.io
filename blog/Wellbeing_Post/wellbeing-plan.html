Wellbeing Implementation Reflection

Through out bootcamp I have kept wellbeing at the forefront of my mind. I have kept up with physical excercise by joining most yoga sessions and going to the gym. I have worked hard to eat breakfast every morning and the supply on campus has definitely come in handy for days where I am running a little late. I have used my time on the bus to listen to music. I have spent my weekends doing bits and pieces to stay on top of my work load but also to see my friends either in person, or virtually. I have continued to eat dinner with my family and enjoy some board games every now and then. I have used my lunch breaks to take walks in the domain and get some fresh air and sunshine. I truely feel this has helped me stay optomistic during bootcamp and has contributed to feeling grounded and not feeling over-whelmed.
